Moving forward in every sense
The redesigned exterior elements—from hood to door mirrors to door handles—have been purposefully designed to minimize resistance and maximize airflow from windshield to cab.

Bringing the outside in
The available Camera Monitoring System (CMS) can reduce drag by replacing the classic side mirror with aerodynamic exterior cameras and intuitively-placed video monitors inside the cab.

Low ground effects, front wheel closeouts, new cab side and roof fairing extensions, and a reduced-height front fender work together to lessen drag and increase fuel efficiency.
